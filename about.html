MACH II: The covers band for you.

Mach II are a good old rock band playing everything from classic seventies bangers through to hits of the nineties and beyond. We love to play great music all night long and we're passionate about guitar driven rock music!

    Mark is our frontman. Armed with a powerful voice, tambourine and occasionally a harmonica - he can sing all the way from a silky smooth harmony and easily shift to a roar to belt out them anthems.

    The other Mark is our lead guitarist. From good old rock to modern bangers he shreds away keeping the songs smooth and tight.

    Jon is our rhythm guitarist. He's got a mean Fender Strat that he uses to keep the rhythm tight while adding his own flourish.

    Ray is our bassist. Keeping a strong steady bassline in time with our drummer, he's essential for giving the music its groove.

    Nic is our drummer. The buck stops with him when it comes to keeping our tunes on time. He's an accomplished and seasoned drummer with years of gigging experience.
